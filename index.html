About

Greetings to my generally fan art page. My name is Mikey Williams and I enjoy sketching and drawing out fan art mostly and some original content too. I enjoy sketching, inking, alot of sharpie work, and have started to dabble with color pencil.

I love art as a hobby, so fan art is a great way to express myself, without getting too attached to anyone character to call my own and needing to keep them alive, past their welcome.

Info

  • What is your favorite art style?

    I do not have a preferred style as it goes. I love to mimic and chameleon in a style. I am working on looking at work, and channeling a different phase on top of that works shapes. So say you have a comic book style super hero, I would then go for a pop art style or a cubism style instead of the original appearance. That is my new goal.

  • Why do you like Art?

    Art is freedom, creative, expressive, and therapeutic. You have no bounds expect the bounds you place on yourself or what you feel should be there. It is one of those spaces where rules are only learned to be broken and expression is everything.
